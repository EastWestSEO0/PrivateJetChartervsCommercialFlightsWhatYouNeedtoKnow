What is an Aircraft Rental Service?

An aircraft rental service allows individuals or businesses to hire an aircraft for private use. Unlike commercial flights, where passengers are restricted by fixed schedules and routes, renting a jet offers complete flexibility. This service includes options such as private jet charter, executive air charter, and aircraft charter, catering to both domestic and international travel.
